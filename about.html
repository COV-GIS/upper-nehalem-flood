Who We Are

Upper Nehalem Flood is a partnership of local governments and volunteers committed to flood hazard education and preparedness in the Upper Nehalem Valley with the following goals:

  • Promote flood hazard awareness and preparedness.
  • Collecting and analyzing data from flood events.
  • Providing flood information and data products specific to the Upper Nehalem Valley.

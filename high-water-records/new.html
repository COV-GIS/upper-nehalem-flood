Add High Water Record

This application with walk you through the process of submitting a high water record for a location with the Upper Nehalem Valley. The information collected will be analyzed with other datasets to improve flood modelling and data products.

Only one record for a specific location and flood event may be submitted at a time. If you have multiple records to submit, you will have an opportunity to summit additional records after completion.

Your Information

We will never share or make this information public. We may contact you via email to follow up or get more information about your submittal.

Location Information

Navigate to the location in the map below and click to choose a location. Please be as precise as possible. If the location of the high water mark is a structure, place the point on a flat surface, such as a driveway, adjacent to the structure.
